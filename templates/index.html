Registers contain facts that the law requires government to record that meets user needs and can be cited as the official source of truth. Example registers include: address, country, postcode, post-town, and school.

Here you can see demonstration applications built by developers using registers.

Every register consists of a set of fields such as: name, text, postcode, longitude, latitude, start-date, and end-date.

The values which may appear in a field are constrained by its datatype. Example datatypes include: string, integer, decimal-degrees, and curie.

This alpha is being developed by a small team at the Government Digital Service as open source software. We welcome contributions and feedback.
